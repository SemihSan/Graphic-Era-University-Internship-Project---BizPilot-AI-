Document type: pricing

## English

### Pricing Tiers

#### Starter

- Monthly price: USD 49
- Best for: small teams testing AI-assisted business development
- Includes: company-document Q&A, basic lead scoring, and 100 outreach drafts per month
- Support: email support

#### Growth

- Monthly price: USD 149
- Best for: growing sales and marketing teams
- Includes: everything in Starter, competitor summaries, custom lead-scoring rules, and 1,000 outreach drafts per month
- Support: priority email support

#### Pro

- Monthly price: USD 399
- Best for: larger teams that need deeper automation
- Includes: everything in Growth, advanced analytics, RAGAS evaluation reports, and 5,000 outreach drafts per month
- Support: priority support and onboarding session

### Discount Policy

- Annual billing discount: 15 percent
- Student or academic demo discount: available on request
- Custom enterprise pricing: available after discovery call

### Notes

Pricing is sample data for the internship MVP and should be used only for RAG testing.

## Türkçe

### Fiyatlandırma Paketleri

#### Starter

- Aylık fiyat: USD 49

- İçerik: şirket dokümanı Q&A, temel lead scoring ve ayda 100 outreach taslağı

- Aylık fiyat: USD 149

- Destek: öncelikli e-posta desteği

#### Pro

- Aylık fiyat: USD 399
- En uygun kullanım: daha derin otomasyon isteyen büyük ekipler
- İçerik: Growth paketindeki her şey, advanced analytics, RAGAS evaluation reports ve ayda 5,000 outreach taslağı
- Destek: öncelikli destek ve onboarding session

### İndirim Politikası

- Yıllık ödeme indirimi: yüzde 15
- Öğrenci veya akademik demo indirimi: talep üzerine
